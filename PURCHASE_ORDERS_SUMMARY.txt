🔒 STOCK SYNCHRONIZATION:

   When Stock IS Updated:
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   • "Save & Mark as Received" (create)
   • "Receive Goods" (detail page)
   
   Process:
   1. Call receive_goods RPC
   2. For each item:
      - log_inventory_movement(product_id, 'purchase', +qty)
      - UPDATE products SET current_stock = current_stock + qty
      - INSERT INTO inventory_movements
   3. Update PO status automatically
   
   When Stock is NOT Updated:
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   • "Save as Draft" (create)
   • Edit draft PO
   • Cancel PO
